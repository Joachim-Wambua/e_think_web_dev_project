TEDxALURwanda 2019 - Dare to Disrupt

By Joachim Wambua & Atoni Araka October 25, 2019 No Comments

The inaugural TEDxALURwanda event organized on October 25th 2019 was entirely organized by the students at the African Leadership University. The event was themed Dare to Disrupt which was aimed at inspiring the youth to take up the mantle and become real change makers in their societies.

The pool of speakers was full of inspiration with great words of wisdom.

The E-Think team was once more responsible for covering the event’s live social media as well as coordinating and setting up the tech department making sure every required device was fully functional and present whenever required.

The E-Think Team also runs TEDxALURwanda’s social media accounts and come up with regular concepts for ensuring constant engagement between the social m=platforms and the intended target market.
