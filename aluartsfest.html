ALU Arts Festival 2019 // Theme -ProvocARTive

By Joachim Wambua April 20, 2019 No Comments

During this three-day arts festival event, E-think Creative played an important role in creating authentic hype prior to the event as well as deliver a unique live digital coverage experience for the participants.

Our team designed and produced digital marketing content as well as physical content such as posters, banners, t-shirts, wristbands, keyholders and more. The E-think team also supervised and guided the participating artists in aligning their presentations in a bid to make them more compelling and engaging.

The team also curated social media content for live-tweeting as well as for future use. The overall design of the venue as well as the tech department during the event was handled by our highly skilled E-Think team.

Here is what the ALU Arts festival Project Manager Victor Kagimba had to say, “These guys are a young team full of spirit and creative energy. From the way they collaborate to the way they handle high pressure situations is simply inspiring to see from such a young team.”
