We Live To tell compelling Stories for visionary African brands

E-Think was founded in 2019 and is a full-service creative agency with a niche focus in business re/branding using novel digital tools such as programming, digital marketing, as well as helping African brands tell their stories effectively.

One of our most valuable assets exists in our team's ability to merge disruptive digital and artistic innovation efforts to accelerate our clients to reach their full potential, through launching or expanding into new markets, improving sales and crafting fortified brand awareness strategies. Ideally, our core purpose as an organization is to design experiences with the optimum purpose for the client's benefit..
